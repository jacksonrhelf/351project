This page is about Old School RuneScape.

What is OSRS?

OSRS is a medieval MMORPG built around resource gathering and skill progression.
Players progress 23 different skills in order to improve their gear, earn money, kill stronger monsters, and complete quests.

This is my character.

Screenshot by Jackson Helf, 2020.

Lately I have been mostly playing the ironman and Leagues gamemodes of the game. In ironman mode, players are not allowed
to trade with other players and thus must get all of their materials on their own. The Leagues gamemode is similar to ironman
mode but provides a number of modifiers to the game to make things faster and more interesting.

I especially enjoy OSRS because has a great sense of progression as well as the fact that I am usually able to do other things while
I play it, like watch lectures, do homework, or watch movies. Admittedly, due to being inside a lot due to quarantine, it has become one of my biggest hobbies!
