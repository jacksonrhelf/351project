Madison, WI

This page is about the town that I currently live in, Madison, WI. Madison is located in the south central region of Wisconsin and is the state's
second largest city after Milwaukee. Madison is often noted for its natural beauty due to its location in the middle of a number of scenic lakes,
namely Mendota and Monona. It is also famous for the university that I attend, the University of Wisconsin-Madison. UW-Madison is often recognized
as one of the top universities in the country. All of this combines to make Madison a great place to live.

Madison is unique in that it has the makings of both a big and small town. The downtown area is always active and busy, but taking
just a two mile walk in any direction will lead you into quaint and quiet neighborhoods. There is always something to do in Madison, from
enjoying a pitcher at the Memorial Union Terrace to jumping around at a Badger football game. If you have never visited the city, it is absolutely worth taking a drive to spend a day in Madison!
